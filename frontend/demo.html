🎙️ Voice Booking Assistant

Click the button in the bottom-right corner to start a voice call with your AI appointment booking assistant.

  • Real-time speech recognition
  • AI-powered conversation
  • Natural voice responses
  • Auto parameter collection
  • Appointment scheduling
  • Embeddable anywhere

Widget Configuration

Pass this in the X-Customer-Key header — required to initiate calls.

<script
  src="booking-widget.js"
  data-api-url="https://your-domain.com"
  data-customer-key="ck_live_..."
  data-theme="light"
  data-position="bottom-right"
  data-primary-color="#4F46E5"
></script>

The widget loads automatically — look for the microphone button in the bottom-right corner.
